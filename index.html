Zijing "Jimmy" Hu

I am a doctoral student in Quantitative Marketing at Mays Business School, Texas A&M University. I obtained my master's degree in Business Analytics from Brandeis University and my bachelor’s degree in Marketing from University of International Business and Economics.

My research interests focus on market research methodology and platform strategy. I employ interdisciplinary methods in my projects, including econometric model, deep learning, reinforcement learning, and game theory.
